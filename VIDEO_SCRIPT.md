> Now the AI reasons — this is its own reading of her data: Meena is managing high fixed costs like rent and E M Is, leading to a very low savings rate and signs of financial stress. So instead of selling her a product, it chooses the Stress Shield journey — and suppresses all cross-sell. Knowing when NOT to sell is a decision our agent makes.

> Before anything is sent, a deterministic governance gate — plain code, not the AI — verifies D P D P consent, the TRAI nine-to-nine contact window, and frequency caps. The AI proposes; policy disposes. Every step lands on an explainability audit trail.
