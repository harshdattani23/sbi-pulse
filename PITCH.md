## The 60-second version (elevator)

> SBI has ~100 million people in YONO but talks to them like a billboard — a third of new
> accounts go dormant within a year, and products-per-customer is stuck at 2.3. **SBI Pulse**
> is an agentic AI that senses each customer's behaviour, financial patterns and life events,
> and runs proactive, adaptive engagement **journeys — not nudges**. It anticipates the need,
> starts a multi-step conversation in the customer's own language, **adapts when they reply**,
> and **knows when not to sell** — on financial stress it suppresses the cross-sell and offers
> help. It's consent-native and fully auditable, and it lifts positive outcomes **+20pp versus
> a holdout**. That's Pillar 3, Digital Engagement, done as a real product.

## Anticipated Q&A

- **"Is this real or mocked?"** — The agent loop, journey orchestration, scoring, guardrails,
  vernacular, audit and uplift are real code. Data is synthetic (zero PII by design).
  Productionising = swap the synthetic feed for consented **Account Aggregator** + YONO events.
- **"How is this not creepy?"** — Consent-native (DPDP), one-tap opt-out, restraint on distress,
  and it's optimised on **uplift, not clicks** — the anti-dark-pattern guarantee, quantified.
- **"Why won't a startup just build this?"** — Journey-level engagement needs SBI's transaction
  depth and 100M base; and the compliance spine (DPDP/TRAI/audit) is the hard part most skip.
- **"LLM hallucination / doing the wrong thing?"** — The LLM proposes; a **deterministic** policy
  engine disposes. Money/high-value actions are human-in-the-loop. Auditor-readable.
- **"Scale?"** — Rides YONO 2.0's stated gamification + hyper-personalisation; phased rollout
  to IVR + 22 languages.
